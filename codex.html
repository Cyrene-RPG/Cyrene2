Origins & Past Lives
Your character’s Origin defines what they are at a fundamental level—their biology, instincts, and the unseen forces that shaped them long before they ever set foot in Cyrene. Some are born into structured systems, raised among steel towers and neon-lit streets. Others come from fractured bloodlines, carrying traits that don’t quite belong to any one world. Your Past Life reflects who you were before everything changed. It represents your training, your habits, and the experiences that continue to influence how you react to the world around you. Together, these choices shape not just your abilities—but how the world responds to you.
Choosing Your Origin
You will select one Origin during character creation.
  • Cannot be changed later
  • Determines your natural traits
  • Influences how characters react to you
  • Unlocks unique interactions

Important Note
